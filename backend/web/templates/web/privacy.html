Privacy Policy

Last updated June 2026

DevSync stores only the portfolio information you choose to provide — your projects, skills, experience, education and certifications — to render your dashboard and public portfolio.

What we collect. Account details (email, name), the portfolio content you create, and aggregate analytics about who views your public portfolio (approximate location, device type, referrer).

How we use it. To operate your dashboard, generate your résumé, render your public page and show you view analytics. We never sell your data.

Your control. You can edit or delete any portfolio item at any time, and export or remove your account from Settings.
